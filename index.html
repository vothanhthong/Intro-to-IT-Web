Project Ideas

Lorem Header

After the discussion , the team come to the decision of a outfit costumize website that enables users to custom their own clothes. The website would include different types of clothes (or at least the most popular clothes pieces nowadays) with the customization including colors, shapes, small pieces of accessories, ... As the website’s purpose is to offer users chances to customize their own clothes according to their hobbies, it would also require a design department in order to create a successful website. This appearance is very important with this kind of website. The reason we think this website is interesting is that until now, there are still very few websites that enable users to customize their own clothes. This website give user opportunity to put their own style into the clothes. Everybody likes to own at least one piece of clothes that is unique and show their characteristics, their styles which no one can buy or find. The website would include a task bar with a large screen that presents the clothes design. The task bar would be placed on top of the website with different types of clothes such as pants, t-shirts, shirts, sweaters, ...

As the users access the website, the first step is to choose what type of clothes they want to customize. As they click on the kind of clothes, the most basic image of that clothes would be presented on the customizing screen. At the bottom of the screen, at the same time, there will be a customizing bar displayed which might include colors for different parts of the clothes, shape, accessories and some simple images that users can choose to put on the clothes. The user can also insert the pictures from their devices to put on the clothes as their want according to their own style. We offers two main printing methods, screen printing and direct-to-garment (or digital) printing. Screen printing is the art of applying layers directly to a t-shirt, and direct-to-garment printing is handled by a garment printer that carefully applies ink to shirts. We promise to always use the best print method for your order’s unique needs.

After finishing customizing their clothes, users can have a look at their finished product and decide to go to the payment process or not. If they have not yet satisfied with the products customized, users can always go back to the previous steps and start customizing their products again. If they have satisfied with the product and choose to pay for the clothes they make, customers can carry out the payment step and choose to take the product at the store or to have their products shipped. Other details needed to be included on the website probably the brand’s name (an unique name for the brand so that the customers can introduce to their friends if they want), contact details, including telephone number, email, address so that users can contact if they have any problems or questions.

Also, there will be a report and contribution part for customers to send their reviews and comments to improve the experience for the users. There should also be a reviews part where customers can access and read reviews from other customers about the products as for online shopping, it is always very important to refer to comments and reviews before deciding to purchase something, especially clothes (customers always consider materials and the shape of There would be some foreseeable risk on the building stage of the project and the outcome as well. By hosting on GitHub, there will be no maintaining fee for the website. As the team have not had much experience, the coding member of the team had to learn new coding language to build the web. Furthermore, about the site responsive, we have to work on the desktop stylesheet and then the mobile styling later. At this stage, it would be difficult to rearrange layout of the elements. Our team still try our best to make the website standout from the other similar existed website and suitable for many user objects.
